# Objective

To learn how to describe different qualities about things.

# Grade-1

## Describing Words

Describing words tell us about the noun. It describes the quality of the naming words. Some examples of describing words are:
1. *Smart* teacher
2. *Big* house
3. *Green* parrot
4. *Round* ball
5. *Pretty* girl
6. *Two* boys
7. *Thundering* clouds
8. *Sweet* food
9. *Soft* toy
10. *Loving* parents
