## Identify

(pronoun-2)

Identify which of these words are pronouns and which are not.

Pronoun

```
He, She, It, They
I, You, Them, Us
Him, Her, We, Them
```

Not a Pronoun

```
Sleep, Walk, Run, Sit
Stand, Talk, Jump, Bump
Drive, Car, Jam, Dog
```
